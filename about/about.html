Beginning

Beginning in 1999, librarys.life was founded on the ideals of free and open access to knowledge and opportunity for everyone. We have worked hard to ensure that our users have access to the services and resources they rely on. We are collaborating with network partners to innovate ideas and technologies to make information more accessible.

Simple & useful info

Our goal is to provide concise and high-quality information to the public. In the expanding digital realm, the Internet provides people with a vast amount of data in a disorganized manner, which makes audiences impossible to process information effectively. We filter and offer readers a new way to review contents to help them discover its wide range of resources and services.

Thank you

Because of the support received from all sides in the past decades, we have continued and developed. Out of all of them, you are the one we want to thank the most. This program is contributed by supporters like you. Thank you!
